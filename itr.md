# ITR Filing Guide: FY 2025-26 

A quick-start guide for filing Income Tax Returns (ITR) specifically tailored for salaried employees at Amazon India with an annual income of **₹2.9 LPA**.

## 📌 Overview
For **Financial Year 2025-26** (Assessment Year 2026-27), an income of ₹2.9 LPA falls below the basic exemption limit (₹4 Lakh under the New Tax Regime). 

> **Note:** While tax liability is ₹0, filing a **Nil ITR** is recommended for income proof, visa processing, and claiming TDS refunds.

## 🛠 Prerequisites
Before starting the filing process, ensure you have the following:

- [ ] **PAN & Aadhaar:** Must be linked.
- [ ] **Form 16:** Download from the Amazon Employee Portal (usually available in June).
- [ ] **AIS/TIS:** Downloaded from the [Income Tax Portal](https://incometax.gov.in).
- [ ] **Bank Validation:** Ensure your primary bank account is pre-validated for refunds.

## 📑 Filing Details

| Category | Detail |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Assessment Year** | 2026-27 |
| **ITR Form** | ITR-1 (Sahaj) |
| **Tax Regime** | New Tax Regime (Default) |
| **Due Date** | July 31, 2026 |

## 🚀 Step-by-Step Instructions

1. **Login**
   - Access the [e-Filing Portal](https://incometax.gov.in).
   - Use your **PAN** as the User ID.

2. **Initiate Filing**
   - Go to `e-File` > `Income Tax Returns` > `File Income Tax Return`.
   - Select **AY 2026-27**, **Online**, and **Individual**.

3. **Verify Pre-filled Data**
   - Amazon reports salary data directly to the IT department. 
   - Cross-check the auto-filled salary and interest income against your **Form 16** and **AIS**.

4. **Confirm Tax Liability**
   - Under the New Tax Regime, income up to ₹12 Lakh (including rebates) is tax-free.
   - Your **Total Tax Liability** should show as **0**.

5. **E-Verification (Final Step)**
   - Once submitted, choose **e-Verify Now**.
   - Use **Aadhaar OTP** to complete the process.
   - *Warning: Failure to verify within 30 days renders the filing invalid.*
