# 🎓 Public Universities in Germany: Master's in Business Analytics & Logistics
### Focus: 100% Tuition-Free for Non-EU Students (Winter 2027 Intake)

This list highlights **public universities in Germany** that do not charge tuition fees to international students.  
You only pay a **Semester Contribution** (approx. €250–€400), which covers administration and a public transport ticket.

## 📊 Admission Insights for Engineering Graduates

- **Most Accessible**:  
  - **HWR Berlin** and **University of Hamburg** → Higher acceptance rates, especially for applicants with professional backgrounds (e.g., Amazon logistics experience).
  
- **Highly Competitive**:  
  - **RWTH Aachen** and **University of Cologne** → Very selective, prioritizing near-perfect GPA and strong quantitative coursework.

- **Numerical Restriction (NC)**:  
  - Most programs are **NC (Numerus Clausus)**, meaning admission is restricted to a fixed number of seats and based on a point system
