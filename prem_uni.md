This list features **public universities in Germany** with **zero tuition fees** for international students.  
You only pay a **Semester Contribution** (approx. €250–€400), which usually includes a public transport ticket for the entire state.

## 📊 Admission Insights for Amazon Professionals

- **Logistics Advantage**:  
  Your current role at **Amazon** is a major asset for **TU Darmstadt** and **HWR Berlin**, as they value practical experience in supply chain optimization.

- **Engineering Pivot**:  
  As a **B.Tech Civil Engineer**, you already have the quantitative foundation. Highlight your **Math/Statistics credits** to strengthen eligibility.

- **Numerical Restriction (NC)**:  
  These programs are competitive. Admission is based on a **point system** (GPA + Work Experience + Math Credits).

## 💡 Strategy for Winter 2027

1. **VPD Requirement**:  
   For **Uni-Assist schools**, apply for your **VPD (Preliminary Documentation)** by **January 2027**.

2. **Language Exams**:  
   Take your **IELTS (6.5+)** or **TOEFL (90+)** by **late 2026**.

3. **APS Certificate**:  
   If applying from **India**, secure your **APS Certificate** at least **6 months before the deadline**.
