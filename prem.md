## ✅ Why These Universities Were Chosen
1. **Public & Tuition‑Free** → All listed universities charge only semester fees (€150–€350), no tuition.  
2. **English‑Taught** → Programs are fully or majority English‑taught, suitable for international students.  
3. **Relevant Fields** → Direct focus on **Business Analytics, Data Science, Logistics, and Operations Research**, aligning with your interest.  
4. **Eligibility with 67% B.Tech Civil Engineering** → Mid‑tier public universities (Münster, Cologne, Dortmund, Hamburg, Magdeburg) are realistic with your GPA.  
5. **Competitive Options** → TUM and RWTH Aachen are globally ranked but require stronger profiles (higher GPA or GRE). Added for completeness if you want to test your chances.  
6. **Program Requirements** → All require quantitative background (math, statistics, programming). Civil engineering is acceptable if you highlight quantitative coursework and strengthen with certifications (Python, SQL, Data Analytics).
